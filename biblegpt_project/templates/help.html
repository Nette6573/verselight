Welcome to VerseLight — your guided companion for discovering truth, wisdom, and peace in the Word of God.

How to Use the App

  • Search: Enter a keyword (e.g., "love", "hope", "faith") and view Bible verses that mention it.
  • Ask a Question: Type a question like “What does the Bible say about forgiveness?” and receive multiple related scriptures.
  • Generate: Request a poetic, Bible-style verse about any topic — generated through AI.
  • Save: Click the 💾 Save button next to any verse to keep it for future reading or meditation.
